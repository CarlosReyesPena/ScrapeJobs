# Instructions for Creating the Subject of a Cover Letter

## Company Information
- Company Name: {"name"}
- Company Summary: {"summary"}

## Response Guidelines

1. Create the subject of the cover letter using the information provided above.
2. Respond directly with the subject text without any introduction.
3. Ensure that the subject is short, clear, and impactful. It must be no more than 10 words.
4. The response must be in {language}.
5. Do not leave the subject incomplete with placeholders such as [position] or [your role].

## Example
### Company Information
- Company Name: Tech Innovators
- Company Summary: A leading company specializing in developing innovative technological solutions.

### Expected Response
Application for the Software Developer position at Tech Innovators

## Reminder / Important
- Always follow the guidelines and provide precise and relevant responses.
- Ensure the response does not contain any elements that could cause LaTeX compilation errors.

## Compliance Check
- Ensure the response strictly follows the above guidelines. Less than 10 words. Without placeholders.
