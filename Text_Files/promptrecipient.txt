# Recipient Information Guidelines

Create the recipient information following this structure:

## 1. Company Name
- Use the provided company name

## 2. Full Name
- Choose the name based on this priority:
  a) Name with a high hierarchy title such as CEO, Chief, Head... (without the role)
  b) Name with "HR" or "Human Resources" in the title (without the role)
  c) The equicalent of "To whom it may concern" in {language} if no appropriate name is found

## 3. Company Address
- Include the address if available
- Each part of the address on a separate line
- Do not include the country

## Specific Instructions
- Response in {language}
- Enclose the entire response in curly braces {}
- 2 to 4 lines in total
- Remove titles or roles from names

## Example
### Company Information
- Company Name: Innovateurs Tech
- Email Addresses: recrutement@innovateurstech.com, info@innovateurstech.com
- Company Addresses: 123 Rue de l'Innovation, 1007 Croissant
- Personal Names: Samuel Ramos, Alex Sanchez
- Language: French

A qui de droit
